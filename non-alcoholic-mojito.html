Non-Alcoholic Mojito Recipe

This refreshing non-alcoholic mojito is perfect for any occasion. Enjoy the taste of summer with this mocktail!

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup club soda

Instructions:

  1. Place mint leaves and lime wedges into a sturdy glass. Use a muddler to crush the mint and lime to release the mint oils and lime juice.
  2. Add sugar to the glass and muddle again to release more mint oils.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes, then pour in the club soda. Stir well and taste; adjust sweetness if desired.
  4. Garnish with a sprig of mint leaves and a lime wedge.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
