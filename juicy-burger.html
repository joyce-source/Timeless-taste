Juicy Burger Recipe

This juicy burger recipe will satisfy your cravings for a classic American meal. Fire up the grill and get ready to enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, chopped onion, breadcrumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape into burger patties.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Grill the burger patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom half of the buns. Add your favorite toppings and cover with the top half of the buns.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your juicy burgers!
