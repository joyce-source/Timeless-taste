Banana and Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

This delicious and nutritious smoothie is perfect for a quick and easy breakfast or snack. Enjoy the refreshing flavors of banana and blueberry!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Peel the banana and place it in a blender along with the blueberries, yogurt, milk, honey (if using), and ice cubes.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
  4. Optional: Garnish with additional blueberries or banana slices.
