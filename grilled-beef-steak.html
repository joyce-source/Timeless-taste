Grilled Beef Steak Recipe

This grilled beef steak recipe will give you perfectly tender and juicy steaks every time. Fire up the grill and get ready for a delicious meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 beef steaks (such as ribeye or sirloin), about 1 inch thick
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika to make a marinade.
  2. Place the beef steaks in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Use your hands to rub the marinade into the steaks, coating them evenly.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and let the steaks marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or up to 24 hours.
  4. Preheat your grill to high heat. Remove the steaks from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the steaks for 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Use a meat thermometer to check for doneness; the internal temperature should read 135°F (57°C) for medium-rare, 145°F (63°C) for medium, or 160°F (71°C) for well-done.
  6. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Slice the steaks against the grain and serve hot. Enjoy!
