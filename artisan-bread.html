Artisan Bread Recipe

This is a basic recipe for making delicious artisan bread at home. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, water, yeast, and salt. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the dough rise at room temperature for 12-18 hours, or until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a Dutch oven or baking dish with a lid in the oven to preheat.
  4. Once the dough has risen, turn it out onto a floured surface and shape it into a round loaf. Place the loaf on a piece of parchment paper.
  5. Remove the preheated Dutch oven from the oven and carefully place the dough into the pot using the parchment paper as a sling. Cover with the lid and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. After 30 minutes, remove the lid and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  7. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving.
